## 🧠 About Me

I don't just study computer science — I obsess over *why machines learn* and *how they fail*. Most of my late nights are spent reading papers, breaking models, and figuring out what went wrong.

Started with C and low-level thinking. Now I'm building on top of Python and ML frameworks — but the systems mindset never left. I care about code that's not just correct, but ***explainable and efficient***.

When I'm not coding, I'm probably arguing about whether AGI is 5 years away or 50. 🔥
